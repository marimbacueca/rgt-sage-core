# RGT Glossary — Full (v3.6.1)

**Reflexivity.** A living tide that learns from its own return.  
**Cadence (α).** Update tempo per period.  
**Gain (K).** Correction intensity per error unit.  
**Rate–capacity (r/κ).** Velocity versus interpretive bandwidth.  
**Λ (lambda).** Closed-loop bound; must remain <1.  
**Audit deficit (D).** Gap to threshold τ; contracts with ρ<1 (or ρ_κ batched).  
**Legitimacy (L).** Consent × performance × fairness × coherence C.  
**Comprehension (q).** Share that can explain policy in ≤60s.  
**Coherence (C).** P·D·(1−D_r) — provenance, digestibility, anti-drift.  
**Rights R*.** Non-tradeable constraints.  
**Mechanism M.** Random-partition peer scoring with DP; truthful aggregation.  
**A_c.** Culture adapter pack (US, CL).  
**AM1–AM3.** Aesthetic Minimums for release gating.  
**EPS.** Epistemic Provenance Score for data integrity.  
**D*.** Digestibility Gate for cognitive load.  
**RPR.** Right to Partial Reflexivity (privacy with DP).  
**G_r.** Stability governor for reflexivity depth/cadence.
